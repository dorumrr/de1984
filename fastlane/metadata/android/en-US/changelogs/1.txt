De1984 Firewall release v1.0.0

Features:
• VPN-based firewall with per-app network control
• WiFi, Mobile Data, and Roaming blocking
• Allow/Block All modes
• Package management (with root)
• Enable/disable/uninstall any package, system included
• Force stop apps, user or system
• Zero tracking or analytics
• 100% open source (MIT License)
